Ambrosia

Overcome the obstacles in order to climb up to the summit of the mountain and get the elixir of immortality!

Fishing Trash

You’re a fish that has to save the world from pollution by starting the garbage from the sea, dodging jellyfish and hungry sharks.

Paradoxhedron

Everyone have a diferent view of the geometry, one will see spheres and the other one cubes. Change the view with a lever to solve the puzzle.
